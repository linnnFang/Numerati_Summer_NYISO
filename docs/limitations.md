# Limitations

- Event-level outage data may be unavailable, preventing construction of a
  valid Negative Binomial outcome.
- P-15 reports generation outage MW, not event count.
- Forced and scheduled outage status may not be distinguishable.
- Facility-to-zone mapping may be incomplete or time-varying.
- Correlated outages and common shocks are not fully described by a simple
  Negative Binomial model.
- Market-rule changes can make the process nonstationary.
- Real-time LBMP and DART may be outage outcomes or jointly determined with
  outages; they should not be presented as simple causal drivers.
